UCU prioritizes workforce enhancement,conducts orientation for non-teaching personnel.

December 15, 2023

With the objective to provide non-teaching personnel with a comprehensive understanding of their duties and responsibilities in the academic community, the administration conducted an orientation program dedicated to all academic and administrative support staff on September 27-28, 2023 held at the UCU Cafeteria.

Regular and Part-Time non-teaching employees were scheduled on the first day, while Casual employees were scheduled for the second day. Topics such as Work Core Values, UCUPA Membership, Health and Sanitation Protocol, and Fringe Benefits, Compensation, Contract, and other personnel matters were discussed in the program.
